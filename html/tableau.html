How are profits faring in each of the 50 states?

Based on the data, the states that have the highest profit are California, New York, and Washington. The states that are losing the most money are Texas, Ohio, Pennsylvania, and Illinois. Most of the states in the midwest and mountain west regions of the United States aren't making high profit. Generally speaking, the closer to the East Coast one gets, the higher the profits become.


How has California fared for the last two years by profit, shown by month?

It's interesting to see the big difference in profit from month to month in the state of Califoria. Typically the months closer to the end of the year have a higher profit. There is also a spike in profit typically around March or April. The higheset earning month was November of 2013, and the profits haven't been close since. The trend shows that even though the profits vary from month to month, there is a general increase.


How are profits within California doing for each product category?

The highest category by far is Office Supplies. The category that comes close is Techology. The third category of furniture is not even close to half as profitable as either the Technology nor Office Supplies categories.

Forecast sales in California for Office Supplies until November of 2015

The forecasted sales show that the profits for the most profitable category, Office Supplies, are expected to rise steadily for the next year. The future profits look to be steady, although not as profitable as some of the months past.
